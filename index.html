How It Works

1. Quantum State Preparation

Random qubits are prepared in different bases (rectilinear or diagonal)

2. Quantum Transmission

Qubits are transmitted and measured in random bases

3. Key Sifting

Matching basis measurements are kept to form the secure key

4. Message Encryption

The generated key is used to encrypt and decrypt messages
